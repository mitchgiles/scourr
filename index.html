Our Story

Born from frustration
with boring scourers.

Scourr
EU / UK

The reusable cleaning pad category has existed for decades. The products work brilliantly — 100% cotton, resin-coated, non-scratch, dishwasher safe. But every single brand in the space decided to make them look like industrial cleaning supplies.

Scourr started with a simple question: what if the sponge under your sink was actually worth showing off? What if it was a gift someone was genuinely pleased to receive? What if it sparked a conversation rather than disappearing into a drawer?

The answer is a brand built around joy, approachability, and the honest truth that cleaning doesn't have to be miserable. Scourr is the EU/UK relaunch of a proven product — with the brand it always deserved.
